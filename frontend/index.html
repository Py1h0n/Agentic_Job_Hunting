Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about The Curator—answered honestly.

help Will my current employer be notified?

No. Stealth Mode is always on by default. Your profile is never visible to your current employer. We use advanced privacy filters that prevent your activity from appearing in LinkedIn searches, email notifications, or any platform your employer monitors.

help What file formats do you support?

We accept PDF and DOCX resume formats, as well as LinkedIn profile exports. Simply upload your existing resume and our AI will extract all leadership achievements, quantifiable results, and career milestones automatically.

help How long does the matching process take?

Initial curation completes within 24 hours. However, quality improves over time as our engine learns your preferences. The longer you use The Curator, the more精准 your matches become.

help Is my data shared with third parties?

Never. Your profile is never shared with employers unless you explicitly initiate contact with a specific opportunity. We never sell or distribute your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

help Can I use this while currently employed?

Absolutely. In fact, the majority of our users are currently employed executives seeking their next move. Stealth Mode was built specifically for this scenario—complete discretion while you evaluate opportunities at your own pace.
